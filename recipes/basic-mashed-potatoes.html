Basic Mashed Potatoes

Description

A simple recipe for mashed potatoes, a thanksgiving staple. Prepare your thanksgiving feast with this simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves of garlic, peeled (optional)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Basic Mashed Potatoes

Description

A simple recipe for mashed potatoes, a thanksgiving staple. Prepare your thanksgiving feast with this simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves of garlic, peeled (optional)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil. Add potatoes and garlic. Lower heat to medium, or simmer until potatoes are tender, 15-20 min.
  3. When potatoes almost done, heat milk and butter in small saucepan over low heat until butter is melted.
  4. Drain potatoes and return to pot. Slowly add warm milk mixture, blending it with a mixer until potatoes are smooth and creamy.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve and Enjoy!
